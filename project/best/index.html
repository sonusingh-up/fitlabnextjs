03 — HOW TO PICK · 4 RULES

If you're buying whey for the first time.

01

Look for "isolate" on the label if you're lactose-sensitive. Otherwise, concentrate is fine and 30% cheaper.

02

Compute cost-per-25g-protein, not cost-per-scoop. The scoop size lies more often than the price.

03

Prefer brands with third-party certification — NSF, Informed Sport, or USP. Uncertified brands fail lab at 4× the rate.

04

Ignore "grass-fed" marketing for protein content. Amino acid profile is identical. The premium pays for sourcing transparency, not nutrition.
